FOOD


AS AN NATIVE SINGAPOREAN, GOOD FOOD IS ONE OF THE MOST IMPORTANT ASPECTS OF MY LIFE. THANKFULLY, BOTH CANADA AND SINGAPORE ARE CULTURALLY DIVERSE AND ARE HOME TO AMAZING FOOD. IN SINGAPORE, FOOD CAN BE DIVIDED INTO 5 CATEGORIES:

  • MEAT
  • SEAFOOD
  • RICE
  • NOODLES
  • DESERTS

THE ABUNDANCE OF STREET-SIDE STALLS AND HAWKER CENTERS MAKE IT AND CHEAP TO GRAB A BITE OF ANY ONE OF THE ABOVE. FURTHERMORE, MANY OF THESE FOOD STALLS ALSO HAVE MICHELIN STARS. FOR EXAMPLE, THE TIAN TIAN CHICKEN RICE STORE IN MAXWELL FOOD CENTRE BECAME INTERNATIONALLY FAMOUS AFTER IT WAS VISITED BY FAOMUS CHEF GORDON RAMSAY. HAWKER CENTRES LIKE THESE MAKE UP MANY HAPPY MEMORIES OF EATING OUT WITH MY FAMILY. LEARN MORE ABOUT THE BEST HAWKER CENTERS BY CLICKING HERE.
